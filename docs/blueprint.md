# **App Name**: GREON: Nature's Green Gold

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure role-based login and signup for users (clients) and administrators, ensuring restricted access based on roles.
- Live Air Quality Dashboard (User): Real-time data visualization of CO₂ concentration, Oxygen levels, daily/total CO₂ absorption, unit efficiency & uptime, with interactive charts and trend graphs.
- Environmental Impact Visualization (User): Display of purified air volume, carbon reduction equivalent visuals, and downloadable environmental impact reports.
- Unit Management (User): Interface for users to view their installed GREON units, including status (active, maintenance, offline), and installation location details.
- Central Monitoring Dashboard (Admin): Aggregation of real-time CO₂ and O₂ data from ALL GREON units, enabling performance comparison and alerts for low algae efficiency, sensor failure, and upcoming maintenance.
- Automated Content Suggestion Tool (Admin): Based on trending environmental concerns, provide admins with content suggestions to incorporate into advertising panels and CSR branding records, ensuring relevance.
- Maintenance Scheduling & Technician Assignment (Admin): Automated generation of maintenance reminders, technician assignment tools, and comprehensive maintenance logs and history.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#386641) to represent nature and environmental friendliness.
- Background color: Light sage (#F0F5F1), almost white, for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Terracotta (#A74A2F) to draw attention to calls to action and to give an earthy and grounded vibe.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for its combination of modern look and a little warmth.
- Use flat, vector-based icons in shades of green and gray to represent various data points and actions.
- Clearly differentiate user and admin portals with distinct layouts, the user portal being impact-focused and friendly, while the admin portal should be data-dense and operational.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience when data updates or navigating between sections.
